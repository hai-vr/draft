I have learned from others, and shared knowledge inside Discord servers.

However, this knowledge is lost to the depths of Discord search, lost in Tweets, and sometimes does not get out.

Knowledge Index is an initiative dedicated to the writing of public searchable knowledge around Virtual Reality and User-Generated Content Social Platforms.

Participating in the public indexing is currently limited to a closed group of participants while we set things up and see if it can work out.
